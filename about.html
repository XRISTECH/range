ABOUT US

GET TO KNOW US

Rage Foundation is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) majorly focused on giving less privilaged childrem within the ages of 3-17years free and qualitative education to enable them become people of substance and value in the society.

MISSION & VISION

Mission

Our Mission at Rage Foundation is majorly focused on giving less privilaged childrem within the ages of 3-17years free and qualitative education to enable them become people of substance and value in the society and not just to the society but the country at large.

Vission

Our Vision at Range to empower at least 600 less privilaged children and youths by 2026 as this will help the society to be better place for us all, as youths engagement is the best way to stop crime and criminality in the society and country at large.
